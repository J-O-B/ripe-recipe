What Is Ripe?

Ripe is a platform for cooking enthusiasts from around the world to connect through their shared interest in food & drinks.

Ripe is a website which allows users, who sign-up for free profiles, to connect with friends or people they don't know, online.
It allows users to share food & drinks recipes, as well as communicate with other users via personal message boards, or through commenting on recipes.
